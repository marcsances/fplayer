Welcome to your music.

urMusik is a lightweight, powerful, fast and simple audio player. Do not expect functions out of the basics in fPlayer. We don't want to. We work in a player that fullfits your needs: an audio player. This way we offer you a lightweight but functional program. Doesn't matter if new or old, with urMusik it will work super-speed.

Nosotros trabajamos en un reproductor que ofrezca lo que necesitas: un reproductor de música. Así, te ofrecemos un programa muy ligero, pero funcional.
